Vaccine Reaction Guidance

Emergency Symptoms

Seek immediate care for: Difficulty breathing, swelling of face/throat, high fever (104°F+), or seizures

Normal Reactions

Common and expected: Mild fever, soreness at injection site, fussiness. Use cool compress and children's pain reliever as directed

Follow-up Care

Schedule follow-up for persistent symptoms or concerns about future vaccine doses
